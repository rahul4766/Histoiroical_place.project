  Taj Mahal is one of the seven wonders of the world. It is recognized
  as one of the most beautiful structural compositions in the world and
  was designated as a UNESCO World Heritage site in 1983.
  
  Shah Jahan built the Taj Mahal in memory of his beloved wife, 
  Mumtaz Mahal, after she passed away. It is said that Shah Jahan 
  gifted the Taj Mahal to his wife. It represents the love and bond
  of husband and wife and is recognized as the symbol of love.
